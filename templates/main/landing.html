Why Tantorial?

We consider ourselves PACE setters and thus our core values are Performance, Accountability, Continuous Learning and Excellence.

We pride ourselves as an online platform that offers a state-of-the-art medium for learners and teachers of all category of study to interact directly with each other. For those interested in learning, we provide trusted tutors. For tutors, we give them a wide range of trusted learner options to choose from. We have a wide community of knowledge lovers.

  • Both learners and tutors can create which shows what they can teach or what they wish to learn .
  • Learners can find and contact tutors directly with a few clicks
  • Learners can create a profile of what they want to learn which will allow tutors find learners.
